Chicken Curry

Description

This dish is called chicken curry. It is made up of chicken, vegetables, coconut milk, and tomato paste

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • vegetables
  • onion
  • coconut milk
  • tomato paste
  • seasoning

Steps

    1.Put onions with butter and olive oil
    2.Then put garlic and mixed vegetables
    3.Put tomato paste then coconut milk
    4.Pur chicken in mixture and let them simmer together with seasoning
