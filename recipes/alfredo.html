Alfredo Pasta

Description

This dish is called pasta alfredo. It is made up of pasta, heavy cream, and mushrooms

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • chicken
  • mushrooms
  • onion
  • milk
  • heavy cream
  • seasoning

Steps

    1.First put the pasta in the water to boil
    2.Add onions and mushrooms
    3.Put milk and heavy cream and let it boil
    4.Add pasta and chicken allow it to simmer with seasoning
